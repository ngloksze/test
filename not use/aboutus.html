About Us

On behalf of the APEC Business Advisory Council (ABAC) and the ABAC Digital Innovation Working Group (DIWG), Digital Innovation Forum presents a forward looking agenda on frontier challenges now impacting on manufacturing, distribution, financial sectors/banking, e-commerce, IT, education and research.

We aim to develop a business-centric framework of principles relevant to the advancement of digitization and innovation, and acknowledging that the future world will be more sophisticated in terms of openness, transparency, the free flow of information and technology agnosticism. It is our vision that the Forum will have global impact on e-commerce, which will contribute $3.6 trillion by 2019. The Asia Pacific region is where most of the growth will take place. The Forum will also generate and promote inclusiveness and address global disruptions.

This is the moment for us to unveil the opportunities and challenges in such a disruptive world.


Hung-Tze Jan
ABAC Chair 2018 ABAC DIWG Chair 2018

About APEC: The Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) is a regional economic forum established in 1989 to leverage the growing interdependence of the Asia-Pacific.

Find out more: https://www.apec.org/

About ABAC: The APEC Business Advisory Council (ABAC) was created by the APEC Economic Leaders to support business-related issues or to provide the business perspective on specific areas of cooperation.
